Who is eligible?

Defence Bank offers financial products and services to not only the Australian Defence Force, but the broader community as well. The bond we have with Australian Defence Force is strong, but we assist family, friends and supporters of the Defence Community as well. And you do not have to leave Defence Bank just because you resign or retire from the Defence Force or move interstate. You can continue to take advantage of our competitive products and services.

Why us?

Defence Bank is one of Australia’s member-owned banks. We have a great range of products from home loans, savings accounts, credit cards, car loans, insurance and many more. We also deliver technology solutions for our members’ convenience to access the services through channels like Mobile, Online and Telephone Banking, or through our Contact Centre.

  • Competitive rates across all products
  • Service above expectations
  • Fee free banking options available
  • Great range of products and services
  • Strong community focus
  • A bank that is dedicated to its members
